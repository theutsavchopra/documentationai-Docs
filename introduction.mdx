## Overview

Bragly simplifies how you create, organize, and share project documentation. You get a centralized space to build wikis, guides, and API references with powerful editing tools and collaboration features. Focus on content while Bragly handles versioning, search, and publishing.

Whether you manage open-source projects, internal team docs, or customer-facing knowledge bases, Bragly scales with your needs.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create a free account at `https://app.bragly.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project" icon="plus">
    Start a new documentation space for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to write your first page. Add Markdown, images, or code examples.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Share your docs publicly or invite collaborators.
  </Step>
</Steps>
