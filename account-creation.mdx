---
title: How to set up your Bragly account?
description: Get started in a few easy steps!
---
1\. To create your free Bragly account, go to <a href="http://bragly.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">bragly.io</a> and click Get Started. You can sign in with Google or email.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/9TXVe0jA26jAxJHlKajW/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/2b8dbb93-b243-4794-be17-b1473c7c91d4/1780407228487.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

2\. If you choose to sign in with Google, just select your account. You'll be led back to the set up page.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/9TXVe0jA26jAxJHlKajW/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/61b03c07-6092-4974-b784-2770190c6fbc/1780407194878.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

3\. Enter your name and your project title and click Start Using Bragly.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/9TXVe0jA26jAxJHlKajW/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/62566a51-c705-4ce0-9cc2-d3efd1655a64/1780407343855.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

4\. That's it! Your Bragly account is set up and your project is ready to use. You get pro-level access to Bragly including unlimited review imports and more for 30 days.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/9TXVe0jA26jAxJHlKajW/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/edd7bdfa-b8da-45dc-8bbf-00ad38150f8e/1780407616820.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

5\. You can create more projects using the Create New Project button. However, the pro-level features are available for only one project during the free trial.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/9TXVe0jA26jAxJHlKajW/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/44182a77-a26b-4708-b1f1-29841ca6a8ec/1780408189723.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />
