---
title: Create and Share a Document
description: Quickly create and share docs in Bragly.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Document">
    Click "New Doc" in your project on Bragly, enter a title and content, then save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the Document">
    Click "Share" on the doc. Copy the link or invite specific users by their email address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

```tip
You control who can view or edit each document you share.
```
