---
title: |
  How to create a Wall of Love on Bragly?
description: A step-by-step guide to creating and personalising your wall of love.
---
1\. If you have set up your project and imported some reviews, you are ready to create a wall of love. It's an engaging collage of reviews in a deeply personalised format.

2\. Navigate to your project home and select Wall of Love from the left-hand menu.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/42ef5d8f-4f86-470e-af47-09fd69a7855a/annotated/1781207041092.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

3\. Click "+ New Wall"

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/3ac8209f-e484-4f21-a7e3-7901830f487d/annotated/1781207041699.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

4\. Get creative! Enter your wall name and URL Slug. Then, Create Wall.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/59dbced6-7f6f-4c7a-b496-7504b43ef1d6/annotated/1781207040842.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

5\. Your wall of love is ready. However, there is a lot of opportunity to personalise it. Try different themes to find one that's truly you.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/f92fc06b-8ea0-4b9d-b212-237ae42077e5/annotated/1781207041242.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

6\. Select Appy Brand Kit to update the wall with your brand color and stylistics. If you don't like the change at any point, use the Reset button to go back.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/5e5c5c90-1b51-4ac2-a25a-39bc02d51ec9/annotated/1781207040916.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

7\. Your page title and subtitle are pre-populated. You can get creative with them or leave them be. Then, simply paste the target link to create a Call to Action Button.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/e0838fac-77e9-47d6-8a50-97a05fa858b1/1780433492670.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

8\. Use toggle switches to easily customize your wall. You can control design elements, number of reviews, minimum ratings, etc. simply by exploring the left-hand menu.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/f5ffaeed-e2b7-48b9-9347-2746e6c41267/annotated/1781207041551.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

9\. Once you are happy with the design, click Save Changes. Your wall of love is ready to use.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/13757256-75ee-4c29-8ec4-15385857fdd5/annotated/1781207041787.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

10\. Now, once again, navigate to Wall of Love using the left panel.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/cbe88ed2-3670-4ee3-a82f-5e160f81ef26/annotated/1781207041484.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

11\. You will see your walls of love listed here. Use the preview button (eye icon) to see what it looks like.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/1070e059-1509-4d23-a938-70a34c7ca84c/annotated/1781207041187.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />

12\. Use the copy link button to get a link to your wall of love. Embed this link into the HTML/Code of your desired webpage. Alternatively, you can connect it to your custom domain and treat the wall of love as a separate page.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/ee0c6e01-c106-471b-bbc1-c8b4f48534e8/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/6d526093-e57d-4c4a-82ae-640d7095f216/annotated/1781207041403.jpeg" width="1920" height="868" />
