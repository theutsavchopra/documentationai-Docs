  kit

\
Updating your brand context and creating a brand kit has many benefits. It allows Bragly to automatically and accurately customize your communication pieces, such as forms and AI-generated replies, to reflect your brand persona.

## Building your Brand Kit

Head to your project home page and select the Brand option on the left menu. This will open the Brand page. Start by uploading your brand's logo. You can drag and drop an image here or browse your device files.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/cGxfwqjS3z6xmOPCVQtO/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/818f63ba-a5f2-4ddb-aa39-23fcfe4cc20b/1781960458641.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

\
Once the logo is ready. Scroll down to set up the colors and the fonts. Any changes you make here are automatically saved. Bragly updates your forms or Wall of Love with the logo and colors you select here whenever you use the Apply Brand Kit button.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/cGxfwqjS3z6xmOPCVQtO/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/aea06ecf-402e-4b78-93f5-7864c0998e2a/1781961023268.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

## Creating your Brand Context

Head to the project home page and select Settings under Workspace on the left menu. This opens your brand page. Start by uploading your company picture. If you need to update the project name at any point, this is where you do it.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/cGxfwqjS3z6xmOPCVQtO/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/ed2da785-0781-4f07-bc0d-c632a4a4a048/1781961074056.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

\
Next, create your brand context. Tell Bragly about your company - your domain, expertise, customer personas - anything that you think Bragly needs to know for creating accurate AI-based outputs, be it a reply or a case study. Once you are done, click Update brand context.

<Image src="https://assets.guidejar.com/uploads/cGxfwqjS3z6xmOPCVQtO/JORzRZHpW5YfHiRSXzSQAsjFu2u1/67f000d8-b025-4458-9947-13aac4cf8f07/1781961205579.jpeg" width="1920" height="1080" />

\
You can use the Smart Context option to create a brand context. Just add your Website URL for the AI to read and generate a brand context. Note: the process may take a few minutes.
